Our Story

Handcrafted with Love,
Shared with the World

From a small studio in Kargil,Ladakh to homes across India — we weave warmth into every stitch.

Who We Are

A Family of Makers

Knitting World was born in 2010 when founder Khatija Akhter began selling handknit shawls at local craft fairs in Jaipur. What started as a weekend passion grew into a thriving community of makers, crafters, and yarn lovers.

Today, we offer over 500 products — from premium Merino yarns to beginner knitting kits — and ship to every corner of India. Every product is chosen with care, and many are still made by hand in our partner workshops.

Our Mission

To make the joy of knitting accessible to everyone — from curious beginners to seasoned artisans — with quality materials and inspiring guidance.

Our Vision

A world where handmade is valued, slow crafts are celebrated, and every home holds something made with love.

Our Story

Fifteen years of stitches, workshops, midnight knit-alongs, and a community that continues to inspire us every single day.
